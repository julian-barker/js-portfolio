Jack of Some Trades

Hi, I'm Julian (He/Him)!


I'm a junior software developer who loves building on the web, learning about new tools and frameworks, and expanding my knowledge into other fields like cyber security, AI/ML, and blockchain / distributed ledger technologies.

About Me

I grew up in southwest Connecticut with my parents and brother. I was always interested in technology and science, but never broke into the self-taught coder life in my youth. After high school, I attended the United States Military Academy, through which I commissioned in 2016 as an Infantry Officer in the US Army, also earning my Bachelors of Science in electrical engineering. I graduated the US Army Ranger School in 2017 and spent the following 5 years in various leadership roles that taught me a lot about leading teams of people, managing valuable resources, and budgeting the most precious commodity of all: time. I recently exited the military (2022) and am now following my dream of learning to code by attending the Code Fellows Software Development bootcamp.

I'm a junior software developer and tech enthusiast. Prior to this, I was an Infantry Officer in the US Army for 6 years and held several managerial and leadership roles during that time. I loved some things about about the Army, but I always felt the itch to to do something more cerebral, and I also realized that the military is neither the only, nor necessarily the best, way for me to serve and do good. That's primarily what drove me to exit the service, but my degree in electrical engineering and my lifelong fascination with computers are really what led me to pursue software development. My proclivity for problem-solving, and that love-hate relationship with being stumped on a tough problem make me a natural fit. I'm now proficient in HTML, CSS, and Javascript and have a firm understanding of basic data structures and algorithms. Some of my greatest strengths are my openness to discourse and criticism, as well as a hunger and ability to rapidly learn new skills and tools. I'm looking for opportunities to contribute and hone my skills in a team of talented devs, and would love to one day touch some of some of our world's greatest problem sets, such as climate change and clean energy, and artificial intelligence.
